Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions.

Mercury is an open-source device farm platform that lets you remotely manage real Android and iOS devices via the browser. It is used for test automation, app development and debugging.
Android phones and tablets, iOS (iPhone & iPad) devices, and Samsung Tizen Smart TVs are supported. Native iOS support is provided on macOS.
Setup is a single command with Docker Compose. Xcode is required for iOS support on macOS. The entire process is documented step by step. On the Enterprise plan, our dedicated team handles the setup for you.
Mercury offers native Appium integration. You can connect to devices through Mercury without modifying your existing test scripts. A detailed integration guide is available in the documentation.
